The Process

When you want to return home, you can find others heading to destinations near yours. Sign up as either a driver or rider and begin your ride today!

The Ride

Find a new friend to join your journey. Matches are created between people with similar interests, so you'll never get bored during the ride. Riding with Better Ways is cheaper than plane, train, or driving alone. With two people, you can split the cost of gas, tolls, and other incurred costs.
